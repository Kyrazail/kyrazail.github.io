Metallica

Metallica is an American heavy metal band formed in Los Angeles in 1981 by the actual frontman and guitarist James hetfield and the drummer Lars Ulrich Since 1981, many lead guitarists and bassists have joined Metallica but the group is nowadays composed by James Hetfield, Lars Ulrich, Robert Trujillo, and Kirk Hammett. Some old members are the following : Dave Mustaine (guitarist), Jason Newsted (bassist), Ron McGovney (bassist), Cliff Burton (bassist, died in a bus crash during a Metallica tour). Metallica is famous for his fast and strength music, and for that reason, they are considered as the leader of the "Big Four of Trash" (one of the four biggest thrash metal bands) with Slayer, Megadeth and Anthrax. Metallica has produced 11 studio albums, 4 live albums, 5 EPs, 25 videoclip and 37 singles. The band has won nine Grammy Awards and five consecutive #1 albums on the Billboard 200, and ranks as one of the highest-grossing bands of all time, with over 200 million records sold worldwide Some of their most famous songs are the followings:

Scorpion

Scorpions is a german hard rock band, created in 1965 by Rudolf Schenker composed by 5 following members : Klaus Meine, Rudolf Schenker, Matthias Jabsb. Their first album was released in 1972. The band has had a worldwide prestige - especially from the 1980s - due to hard rock songs such as No One Like You in 1982 or Rock You Like a Hurricane in 1984 and ballads like Still Loving You in the same year or Wind of Change and Send Me an Angel in 1990, all of them almost systematically commercially successful. In the 1990s, with the decline of the media, the band turned to new experiences, such as Moment of Glory (with the Berlin Philharmonic Orchestra) and Acoustica (a live acoustic album), but returned to their traditional recipes in 2004 with Unbreakable, which was hailed by many fans and was followed in 2007 by the album Humanity - Hour 1. Since then, the band has sold more than 100 million albums worldwide. On January 23, 2010 the band announced on their official website that after their world tour accompanying the release of the new album, they will end their career, but in 2014, they decide afterwards to continue their career !
