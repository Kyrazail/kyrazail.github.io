The guitar


Being a guitarist, I will present you the history of the guitar through the centuries, the most famous guitarists, and the different genres using the guitar.

Description

A guitar is an instrument with six strings arranged in parallel from the headstock to the body. The guitar is composed of 3 parts : The neck, the body, and the headstock. The neck is an important part because the guitarist uses it to determine which notes he plays and in what way (and sometimes plays them directly with techniques like Tapping).
